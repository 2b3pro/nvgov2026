Key Battlegrounds

Washoe County (Reno)

Swing county. Lombardo must flip it; Ford must hold it. Hill's home turf.

Clark County Suburbs

Henderson, Summerlin. Educated moderates who will decide the election.

The "Cow Counties"

Rural Nevada. Lombardo needs 70%+ margins to offset Clark turnout.

GOP Primary

Lombardo faces two challengers: salon owner Irina Hansen and hip-hop artist Matthew Winterhawk. Neither is polling significantly, but they could force Lombardo to defend his right flank.
